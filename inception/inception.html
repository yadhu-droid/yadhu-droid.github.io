 MY TAKE  

Of all Christopher Nolan movies, this was the first movie that I saw. Also, I may argue that this is his best work. "Dream inside a Dream" - This concept makes it a fantasy film. But Nolan made it into a Sci-Fi film. I believe that, it the power of Christopher Nolan. There are various delightful twists and unforeseen turns.

This movie is defenitely rewatchable. Although you know what's coming next, you still feel jumpy about it. The story moves hastily from one scene to another, making the viewers feel like on a roller coaster ride. At times, its hard to keep up, even at second watching. There is a lot of information to recollect and comprehend. In this contemporary age, people are looking for this.

This film may be the prime example of the endless limits of cinema. It's one of the best films of the decade. The visual Effects, Sound Editing, and Sound Mixing are stunning. If you have'nt watched it, you may be missing out. So watch it right now!
