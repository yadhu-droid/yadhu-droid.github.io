 MY TAKE  

I initially saw this movie partly when I was young. But it didnt gave rise to any influence on me (Because 1. I didnt see it completely. 2.I was at 9th grade at the time). At that period, I reckoned it was just a budget film that was struggling to be a sci-fi movie.But it all shifted when I re-watched it wholly.

It may look like a small budget film. But it does have an intriguing story and entertaining characters. But One of the demerit of this movie is that camera work is awful!

This movie concentrates on the several alternate realities that exists and blend them together. There are very few movies that are based on scientific thought experiments and theories that to justice to the thought experiment as well as the storyline. This movie is one of those.

Also, this movie have a diverse critiques. So dont take any reviews (Positive or Negative) into your consideration if you are going to watch it. After watching it you can decide whether you like it or not. Overall its a good movie with an interesting plot.
