ACTOR
Born: Jun 28, 1966, Illinois, U.S.A.
 
An American actor, producer and screenwriter, who has been entertaining audiences since his teens, John Paul Cusack, starred in many teen films in the mid-1980s, one of the popular films being Better Off Dead (1985). His performance in Cameron Crowe`s romantic comedy-drama Say Anything... in 1989 gained him the teen idol status. His other notable movies include High Fidelity (2000), Grosse Pointe Blank (1997), Being John Malkovich (1999), 1408 (2007), Hot Tub Time Machine (2010) and The Raven (2012).
