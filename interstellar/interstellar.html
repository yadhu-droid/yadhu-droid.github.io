 MY TAKE  

I found this movie quite delightful. It apprehends our soul from the first sequence itself. We wont be bored in any part of the movie. Interesting sequences makes us immersed throughout the movie. The storyline of this movie is stunning. It makes good use of Einstien’s “Theory of Relativity”. Its one of the best science fiction film available now.

I am so moved by the movie. The whole concept of relativity is so well demonstrated and explored. Christopher Nolan did an absolute mesmerizing job for this movie. I think i have found myself an all-time classic which i will be rewatching in 30 years or so.

Last but not least, Music by Hans Zimmer is “out-of-the-world” experience. Unlike other Sci-Fi films that does’nt bother about the real scientific background, Interstellar focuses on science. It’s also cool to note that the portrayal of black hole in the movie was approved by scientists.
